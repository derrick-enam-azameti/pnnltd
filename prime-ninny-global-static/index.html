About Prime Ninny Global Group

Prime Ninny Global Group is a fast-growing Ghanaian company focused on providing high-quality essential products and reliable value-added services. We currently operate in three core sectors:

  • Food Manufacturing – Tom Brown, Tiganice (Tigernut blend), and healthy natural food products.
  • Cleaning & Hygiene – Toilet seat sanitizer, hand sanitizer and liquid soap formulated for everyday use.
  • Escrow Services – Trusted and neutral third-party holding service for safe business and property transactions.

Since 2020, we have been committed to delivering products and services that improve everyday living for families, businesses, and communities.
